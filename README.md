# SmartParkIQ : Smart Parking System

**An AI-powered smart parking platform to reduce parking anxiety, overpricing, and vehicle damage**

## Overview

SmartParkIQ is a data-driven smart parking solution designed to help drivers **find affordable, safe, and reliable parking spots in real time**. By combining AI-based pricing prediction, integrated micro-insurance, and seamless navigation support, SmartParkIQ addresses a critical gap in existing parking apps—**parking uncertainty and risk**.

Our core promise:

> **“Never overpay, never worry about parking damage.”**

---

## Problem Statement

Urban drivers frequently face:

* Unpredictable parking prices
* Limited visibility into private parking availability
* Risk of towing or vehicle damage
* Fragmented user experience across multiple apps

Studies show:

* 73% of drivers avoid destinations due to parking uncertainty
* Towing and vehicle damage costs exceed **$13B annually** in the U.S.

Existing solutions focus on availability—but not **risk, trust, or price volatility**.

---

## Solution

SmartParkIQ delivers an end-to-end parking experience by integrating:

* **AI-powered dynamic pricing prediction**
* **Private & public parking inventory**
* **On-demand micro-insurance**
* **Navigation-first user experience**

---

## Key Features

* **AI/ML Price Prediction Engine**
  Predicts real-time parking price surges using Random Forest and Gradient Boosting models.

* **Expanded Parking Inventory**
  Integration with **Air Garage**, providing access to over **40% of private parking inventory** across U.S. cities.

* **Micro-Insurance Integration**
  Optional insurance coverage starting at **$0.50 per transaction**, embedded directly in the booking flow.

* **Google Maps Integration**
  Seamless navigation and contextual parking recommendations.

* **Scalable & Secure Backend**
  Designed to support **10,000+ concurrent users**, 3-second load times, and PCI-compliant payment flows.
